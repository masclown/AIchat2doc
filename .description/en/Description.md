AIchat2doc: 1-Click Export AI Chats to Markdown & Obsidian

Stop manually copy-pasting your AI conversations. AIchat2doc is a lightweight browser extension that instantly converts your chats from popular web-based AI platforms into perfectly formatted Markdown files.

🔥 Why use AIchat2doc? (TL;DR)

Instant Markdown Download: Save any AI response as a clean .md file with a single click—perfect for any text editor, blog, or knowledge base.

Direct Obsidian Integration: Send conversations directly to your local Obsidian vault as new notes.

Lossless Formatting: Flawlessly preserves code blocks, tables, nested lists, images, and text styling.

Universal Support: Works seamlessly across major AI chat interfaces.

100% Private: Runs entirely locally in your browser. No data collection, no remote servers.

Detailed Description:
AIchat2doc provides a seamless bridge between your favorite AI chat interfaces and your personal knowledge management system. By injecting intuitive export buttons right alongside the native copy controls of these platforms, it simplifies the process of preserving valuable AI-generated content.

Beyond simple exporting, the extension intelligently processes the chat content to produce tidy, perfectly formatted Markdown. It also offers customizable automated filename generation based on chat context and timestamps. Designed with privacy and performance in mind, it requires permissions strictly on the supported AI chat tabs.

Key Features:

Wide Compatibility: Works with various leading web-based AI chat interfaces.

One-click Markdown Export: Instantly download AI responses as well-structured .md files for offline archiving or sharing.

Save to Obsidian: Creates notes directly in your chosen vault without interrupting your workflow.

Rich Format Conversion: Maintains the integrity of code blocks, lists, tables, and images during the conversion process.

Smart Button Injection: Adds export buttons contextually beside the default copy controls of your AI chat platforms.

Automated Filenaming: Generates meaningful note titles using chat context or timestamps.

Clean Markdown Output: Intelligently processes the chat elements to ensure the cleanest possible text formatting.

Privacy-focused: Operates exclusively within your browser. No external data transmission or invasive permissions.

How It Works:

Install AIchat2doc.

Chat with your preferred web-based AI platform as usual.

Find the newly added “Download Markdown” and “Save to Obsidian” buttons next to the AI's native copy button.

Click “Download Markdown” to get a universal .md file immediately.

Or, click “Save to Obsidian” to instantly create a formatted note in your local vault.

Privacy:
No personal data is collected. AIchat2doc does not transmit, store, or share your conversations externally. All processing happens locally on your machine.
